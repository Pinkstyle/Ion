Ion

Made in a lab, on a planet called Astrona. Ion is a kind of robot called Eptics, which are assassin robots, which need the blood of their target to operate, this blood is used as their DNA, so anything that happened, or happens to the host of their blood, goes to their memory, even death, after the target is dealt with, they can self destruct, or live "as their target" having the delusion that they're them.


Throughout the course of Ion's "life", He's gotten a bit of a better understanding of human life as he wander around earth searching for his target. Sadly, his programming holds him back from being what he wants to be. Unique
